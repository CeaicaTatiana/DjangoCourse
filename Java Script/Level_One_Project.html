Welcome to my Level One Project!

For this project I will be building a generic website that will seem to ask mundate questions to normal users, but secretly, we are looking for a spy. Anyone visiting this site will be asked a series of questions, but only the spy will be able to give specific answers. If all questions are answered corectly, there will be posted a secret message in a console for a spy.

Here are the four conditions we will check for the spy:

  • The Spy has the same fisrt letter of his First Name and his Last Name
  • The Spy is between the Age of 20 and 30(exclusive of 20 and 30)
  • The Spy is at least 170 centimeters tall
  • The Spy has a pet name that ends with the letter "Y"
